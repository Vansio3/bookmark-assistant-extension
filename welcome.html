Welcome to Bookmark Assistant!

Your intelligent bookmark and history search tool.

1 Pin the Extension for Easy Access

To get the most out of Bookmark Assistant, pin it to your toolbar for a single click. Click the Extensions icon (puzzle piece), find Bookmark Assistant in the list, and click the Pin icon.

2 Master Your Search

The search bar is powerful. Hereâ€™s how to use its different modes:

  • Launch with Shortcuts

    Access the assistant instantly without clicking the icon. Change the default shortcuts, or make them global in the shortcut settings at chrome://extensions/shortcuts

    • Alt + B — Opens the extension in a separate, centered window.
    • Alt + M — Opens the standard extension popup.
  • Standard Search

    Simply start typing to instantly search through all of your bookmarks by title or URL.

  • Filter by Tag #

    Add tags to your bookmarks and then filter them by typing #work or #reading in the search bar. You can even combine tags!

  • Pin bookmarks #pin

    Add the #pin tag to pin your favourite bookmarks to the initial screen.

  • Search Google :

    Start your query with a colon (:) to perform a standard Google search directly from the extension. For example: :how to bake bread

  • AI-Powered Search ::

    Use a double colon (::) to activate a special AI-powered Google search for more complex queries. For example: ::troubleshoot javascript error

  • Toggle History Search H

    Click the 'B' button (or 'H' when active) to switch between searching your bookmarks and your entire browser history.

3 Customize the Algorithm

Fine-tune the search results to match your preferences. Access the Options page by right-clicking the Bookmark Assistant icon in your toolbar and selecting "Options".

  • Adjust Scoring Weights

    You can directly change the importance of different factors in the search results, such as how much weight is given to a word found in a title versus in a URL.

  • Control Usage Bonuses

    Modify how much the algorithm prioritizes bookmarks you've visited frequently or more recently.

  • Reset Learned Data

    The extension learns which websites you prefer over time. You can clear this learned data from the options page to start fresh.
